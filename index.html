About Us

1Objective

Prevent dangerous forest fires and other potential catastrophes with the expert tree and vegetation management services from Volcano Creek Logging Inc. Established 16 years ago, we specialize in healthy forest thinning to keep your property safe and resilient. Our comprehensive services include mechanical thinning, timber harvesting, conventional logging, and thorough tree removal, all designed to maintain the health and safety of your landscape.

2Experience

Since 2000, property owners and government entities have relied on our skilled and adept team for superior clearing and management services. Our knowledgeable staff is dedicated to providing dependable, high-quality solutions that you can trust time and time again. To ensure the utmost standards of quality and safety, the president or vice president of our company is always on-site, personally overseeing each project. Choose Volcano Creek Logging Inc. for unparalleled expertise and commitment to excellence in forest and vegetation management.

3Service Area

Volcano Creek Logging Inc. proudly serves a diverse range of regions across the western United States. Our expert tree and vegetation management services extend to California, Washington, Oregon, Nevada, Idaho, Utah, Arizona, and Montana. Whether you're in the dense forests of the Pacific Northwest or the varied landscapes of the Southwest, our team is dedicated to providing superior forest thinning, timber harvesting, conventional logging, and tree removal services to ensure the safety and health of your property.
